Who we are

Penumbra is a Melbourne based contemporary ballet group with members of all ethnicity's. Founded by renown ballet dancer Meghan Grant, with deep roots in traditional ballet styles and consisting of many of Australia's most talented, Penumbra seeks to revitalize the genre in Australia and compete against the ballet powerhouses of the world.

Meghan Grant

Founder, Dancer, Choreographer

Meghan is the founder and artistic director of Penumbra. Dance teacher at the Melbourne School of Arts and a long time celebrity of the form. She is an advocate of dance and the driving force behind Penumbra
